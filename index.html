Recently news broke in the community that Google had cracked down on unlimited Google Drive.

I have received (daily) the following email. I am currently looking into alternatives, but if there is no route then we will downscale massively in terms of content. Physical media availability sits at around 4TB and Google Drive which we cache is at 52TB. There is no way I can buy this much physical hard drive space and most alternatives are way too expensive, charging for both data bad bandwidth.

Maybe the end of days?
